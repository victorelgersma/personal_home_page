
# Optional Physical Student Card at UU 

Since March 2026, I have been lobbying the University of Utrecht to re-introduce an optional, physical student card so that I can prove my student status without having to carry a smartphone.

If you have questions, comments, feedback, or tips, please e-mail me at v.j.b.elgersma@students.uu.nl
